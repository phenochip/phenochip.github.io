PhenoCHIP

A Phenotype-based Cell Hierarchy and Isolation Platform

About Us

PhenoCHIP is a marker-free enrichment platform for rare cells of interest. PhenoCHIP can deliver a highly enriched, sterile population of phenotype specific cells which are harvested from, and remain, in their native state. This allows in-vivo studies, aggressiveness stagin and drug discovery.
